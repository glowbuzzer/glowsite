# Beckhoff IO

The Beckhoff IO consists of the EK1100, EL1008, EL2008. These clip together to form a unit.

The EK1100 needs two power supplies, one for its own power (Coupler power supply) and the other to power the bus to the other terminals (Power contacts supply).

## Copy from AZBO sensor to drive
1. Click the [Communication] menu of the MEXE02, and select [Copy the ABZO (fixed) information to the driver in a lump].
<Image meta={mexe02_5} alt="mexe02_5" maxWidth={400}/>
2. Select the axis that the motorized actuator is connected, and click [OK].
<Image meta={mexe02_6} alt="mexe02_6" maxWidth={400}/>
3. Click [Yes]. The ABZO information (fixed value) of the motorized actuator is copied to the driver.
<Image meta={mexe02_7} alt="mexe02_7" maxWidth={600}/>
4. After it is completed, click [OK].
<Image meta={mexe02_8} alt="mexe02_8" maxWidth={600}/>
5. Cycle the driver power and check whether the copied value is applied on the unit information monitor window


## Home position setting

1. Switch all power supplies
1. Move the motor shaft to a desired home position by hand
1. Find teh extension switch (little button next to the axis' motor cable connector)
1. Turn on the main power supply and keep the extension switch depressed for one second (the POWER LED (green) and ALARM LED (red) blinks)
1. Release within three seconds of the POWER LED and ALARM LED started blinking, and press the extension switch again within three seconds
1. The home position is now set
